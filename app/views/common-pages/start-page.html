Apply for a maternity exemption

If you are a healthcare professional, you can apply for a maternity exemption for new and expectant mothers.

A maternity exemption entitles the mother to free prescriptions:

  • while she is pregnant
  • for 12 months after the birth of her child

You will need to enter the following on the mother’s behalf:

  • mother’s date of birth
  • mother’s NHS number
  • due or birth date of the child
  • email or postal address
