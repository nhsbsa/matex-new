Maternity exemption account: sign in or register

Use this service to apply for maternity exemption certificates on behalf of new or expectant mothers.

To use this service:

  • You must be a Midwife, GP or Health Visitor.
  • You need to be registered with the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC) or the General Medical Council (GMC) to create an account.
