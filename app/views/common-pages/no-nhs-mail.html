Register for a Maternity exemption account

You must have an NHSmail account, if you don't you'll need to get in touch with your local IT department or administrator.

Once you have an NHSmail account

Email nhsbsa.healthcosts@nhs.net to register to use the Apply for a maternity exemption service.

We'll ask for your:

  • Name
  • NMC/GMC number
  • NHSmail address
  • UK telephone number
  • Primary Care organisation

This should take about 5 minutes.


I'm already registered with this service

Use your NHSmail account details to sign in to the Apply for a maternity exemption service.
