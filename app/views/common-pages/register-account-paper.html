Health care professional Maternity exemption account: Register

Use this service to apply for maternity exemption certificates on behalf of new or expectant mothers.

To register for this service you must be:

  • a Midwife, GP or Health Visitor
  • registered with the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC) or the General Medical Council (GMC) to create an account

Call 0300 330 1341 to register

What we'll ask for
  • Name
  • NMC/GMC number
  • NHS email address
  • UK telephone number
  • Primary Care organisation

This should take about 5 minutes.
