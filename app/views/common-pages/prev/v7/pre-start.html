Health care professional Maternity exemption account: sign in

Use this service to apply for maternity exemption certificates on behalf of new or expectant mothers.

To use this service you must:

  • Have access to an NHS email account
  • Be a midwife, GP or Health Visitor
  • Be registered with the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC) or the General Medical Council (GMC) to create an account
